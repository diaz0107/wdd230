Target Audience

The target audience for this website is nearly everyone who has access to the internet, and either lives in one of the four areas covered or are going to or through them. If a person wants to know the weather in/around one of those areas, they are the targets for this website.

  • Who: Shoppers at Bountiful Ottawa Foods are usually locals from Carlsbad and LDS Full Time missionaries and members of the Church of Jesus Christ of latter-Day Saints.
  • Age: 18 and up.
  • Technology: Most of our shoppers have access to our site through mobile devices like cell phones and I-pads, and senior citizens will probably use home computers most of the time.
  • Income: Income types will vary according to age and levels of education ( $ to a medium of $50,000 or above annually).
  • Motivation: Bountiful Ottawa Foods to offers special holiday menu orders to those around the areas of Carlsbad, California. They can use this site to find menu orders which are available for pick-up or delivery. Local same-day and next-day delivery is available. The business intends to stay focused on meeting the customer’s demand for high-quality, delicious food in a beautiful setting among the vineyard and tasting rooms of Carlsbad, California.
