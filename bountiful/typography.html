Typography


Font One

'Yusei Magic' will be the heading font. It is easy to read and has eye-catching attributes and is a light-hearted sans-serif font.

Font Two

'Lato' will be the paragraph font. This is not as pronounced as 'Yusei Magic', however it has a similar feel to it and both fonts compliment each other well being san-serif fonts. It will also be used in the navigation Bar.
